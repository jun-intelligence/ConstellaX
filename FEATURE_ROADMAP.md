# Feature Roadmap

## Phase 1: Frontend Product Foundation

- Role dashboards.
- AI manager placeholders.
- Campaign management.
- Creator analytics.
- Trust, contracts, payments, notifications.
- Modular workspace builder.
- Future community preview.

## Phase 2: Backend MVP

- Supabase auth and role profiles.
- Postgres schema for users, campaigns, deals, deliverables, contracts, payments, notifications, workspace layouts.
- Row level security by role and relationship.
- Storage for contract and report files.

## Phase 3: Intelligence Services

- Creator scoring.
- Brand compatibility scoring.
- Engagement and audience quality scoring.
- Trend and algorithm insight ingestion.
- AI recommendation generation.
- Campaign report generation.

## Phase 4: Workflow Automation

- Smart reminders.
- Approval routing.
- Payment status tracking.
- Renewal opportunity detection.
- Audit trail generation.

## Phase 5: Community Layer

- Verified profiles.
- Reputation graph.
- Creator circles.
- Brand reliability signals.
- Audience trust and engagement signals.
- Professional discovery and collaboration.
